================================================================================

Depending on the license, you may need to include attribution in your game.
Here are some examples of how to format attributions:

In-Game Credits Screen:
  "Character sprites by [Author Name], licensed under CC-BY-4.0"
  "Music by [Composer Name], available at [URL]"

README or About Dialog:
  This game uses assets created by various artists. See CREDITS.txt for
  complete attribution information.
